  • init(rootRouter:)

    Required init which accepts the RootRouter instance

    The instance of RootRouter may depend on factors determined at runtime, i.e. what device we are running on, what target is selected, whether we are in staging of production. Rather than letting the instance of UIRouter determine its own dependency this method is required to receive an injected dependency.
